# License and Edition Summary

This summary is for users deciding between the free community repository and commercial offerings.

## Editions at a Glance

- Community Edition (this repo): free, MIT licensed
- ATR 6.0 Professional: separate commercial desktop product
- LTX Generative Repair: separate paid add-on for ATR 6.0 Professional

## Community Edition

You can use the Community Edition under MIT terms, including commercial use, modification, and redistribution of repository code.

## ATR 6.0 Professional

ATR 6.0 Professional is sold separately and is not granted by the MIT license of this repository.

## LTX Generative Repair

LTX Generative Repair is sold separately as an add-on for ATR 6.0 Professional and is not included with the Community Edition.

## Decision Guide

- Choose Community Edition if you want the free, open, MIT-licensed branch.
- Choose ATR 6.0 Professional if you need the separate commercial product workflow.
- Add LTX Generative Repair only if you need that specific paid add-on capability.
